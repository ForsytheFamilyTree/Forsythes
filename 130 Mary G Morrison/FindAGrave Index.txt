Mrs. Oliver Rea Dead

Mrs. Oliver Rea, age 32, of LeClaire, the former Mary Morrison of Oskaloosa, died Wednesday
evening in her home from a sudden heart attack. She was born in Blakesburg, Ia., on October
4, 1916. She was married in Ottumwa some 16 years ago and moved to LeClaire just before World
War II. Her husband was employed in a factory in Bettendorf. She is survived by her husband
and five children. Her mother, Mrs. Florence Morrison of Davenport; three brothers, Fallace
Morrison of Cashion, Ariz.; Carl of Davenport; Floyd of Oskaloosa and one sister, Mrs. Ronald
Showman, 511 C avenue east.

From Oskaloosa Daily Herald September 23, 1948 issue
