H. LORENE SHOWMAN

H. Lorene Showman, 60, of 424 N. Fourth St., died Wednesday afternoon, Sept. 26, 1984 at the
Mahaska County Hospital. She was born July 19, 1924 at Searsboro, the daughter of John and
Florence Elder Morrison.

She was married to Ronald E. Showman on June 6, 1941 at Lancaster, Mo. He died Nov. 29, 1973.
She was an Avon representative for 15 years and a member of the First Christian Church and
the VFW Auxiliary.

She is survived by a son, two grandchildren, and one brother, Floyd Morrison of Oskaloosa.

Besides her husband, she was preceded in death by her parents, two brothers and two sisters.

Funeral services will be held Saturday at 11 a.m. at the Newman Funeral Chapel with the Rev.
David Troxler officiating. Burial will be in the Forest Cemetery in Oskaloosa. Visitation will
begin at the funeral home after 9 a.m. Friday. The family will be at the funeral home from 7:30
to 8:30 p.m. on Friday.

Ronald Showman

Funeral services for Ronald E. Showman, 53, 424 North Fourth St., who died Thursday evening at
Veterans' Hospital, Des Moines, will be held at 2:30 p.m. Saturday at the Newman Funeral Chapel
with the Rev. Clinton B. Meininger of First Christian Church, Oskaloosa, officiating. Burial
will be in Forest Cemetery.

Born December 28, 1919 in Delta, he was the son of Samuel and Eva Fuller Showman. He was a
veteran of World War II and an employee of Clow Corporation, Oskaloosa.

Surviving are his wife, Lorene; one son; two sisters, Mrs. Mildred Ewing of Delta and Mrs.
Hazel Deweese of Clay Center, Nebraska; one brother, Robert Showman of Delta.
