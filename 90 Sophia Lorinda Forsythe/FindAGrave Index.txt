MRS. SOPHIA L. ADAMS DEAD

Mrs. Sophia L. Adams died at the home of her son John Adams, north of Drakesville, Wednesday.
Funeral services were conducted this (Thursday) morning by Rev. George Nicol at the John Adams
home at 10:30. Burial was made in the Adams cemetery.

Mrs. Adams was born in Pennsylvania and was 85 years, 1 month and 24 days old at the time of her
death.

From Bloomfield Democrat newspaper January 10, 1929 issue

She and George had three sons; John Quincy, Samuel Lewis, and Francis Clyde.
