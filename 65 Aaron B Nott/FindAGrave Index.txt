Aaron was the son of William Henry Harrison Nott (went by Henry Nott) and his first wife,
Mary Jane Barber. Henry and Mary were married 17 November 1861 in Henry County, Iowa and Henry
enlisted in Company D, Iowa 4th Cavalry Regiment on 25 Nobember 1861. He mustered out on 26
February 1862 at Mount Pleasant, Henry County, Iowa.

Aaron and Bertha spent much of their married life in the area close to where he was born and
grew up.
