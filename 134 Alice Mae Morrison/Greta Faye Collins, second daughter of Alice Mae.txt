The second, and last child, of Alice Mae Morrison and George Collins is Greta Faye Collins. 
She married John Alva Grindle and together they had two daughters and a son.  They divorced and
Greta married second Paul Silknitter, hence her surname in this photo from an unknown local
newspaper.
