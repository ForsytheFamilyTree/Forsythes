Nathaniel (Thannie) Loren Morrison is the sixth-born child of John Thomas and Nancy Rebeca. 
He married Halsie Elizabeth Smith in 1906 in Davis County, Iowa.  They moved west in 1910
eventually settling in Bremerton, Washington in 1919.  They had 8 children, 6 surviving to
adulthood.
