The Coop sisters, circa mid-1950s. 
Rosa May Coop, wife of Joseph Artensus (Tenny) Morrison, is pictured second from left. 
On the left is Effie Ellen Coop, wife of William Morrison, fourth born child of John Thomas
and Nancy Rebeca and brother of Tenny.  Third from left is Anna Coop (wife of Everette Brooke)
and last is Avis Coop (wife of Frank Rose).
