Mrs. Morrison's
Funeral Thursday

Funeral services for Mrs. Rosa Mae Morrison, 85, who died at 5:30 p.m. this morning
[sic] at her residence in Bunch, will be held at 2:30 p.m. Thursday at the Wagler Funeral Home.

The funeral will be conducted by the Rev. Robert Allen with burial in the Mounts Cemetery.
The family will be at the funeral home from 8 p.m. to 9 p.m. Wednesday.

A housewife, she was born to Reuben and Molly Ellison Cook [sic] [should be Coop] on December
29, 1884. She was a lifelong resident of the county and a member of the Pleasant Hill Christian
Church.

She married Joseph A. Morrison.

Survivors include three sons, Ralph Morrison of New London, Homer Morrison of Danville,
Emery Morrison of West Burlington; one daughter, Mrs. Delma Bell of Danville; three sisters,
Mrs. Effie Morrison of Huron, South Dakota, Mrs. Anna Brooks of Bloomfield and Mrs. Avis Rose
of Mount Pleasant; two brothers, Earl Cook [sic] [Coop] of Moulton and Lester Cook [sic]
[Coop] of Ottumwa; 11 grandchildren; and 22 great grandchildren.

She was preceded in death by her husband in 1925, one son and one daughter.

Copied from obituary appearing in July 7, 1970 Davis County Republican newspaper, page 1.
