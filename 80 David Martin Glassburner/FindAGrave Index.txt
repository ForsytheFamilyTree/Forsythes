Glassburners Will
Observe Anniversary

Mr. and Mrs. D. M. Glassburner of Bunch will celebrate their fifty-fourth wedding anniversary on March 28. They make their home on the farm where they went to housekeeping 54 years ago.

Mr. and Mrs. Glassburner have three daughters, Mrs. Jay Hughes of Belknap, Mrs. Jim Moore of Centerville, and Mrs. Winfred Moore of Drakesville. There are eleven grandchildren and six great grandchildren.

No definite plans have been made to observe the anniversary.

Article in the March 25, 1948 issue of the Bloomfield Democrat.
