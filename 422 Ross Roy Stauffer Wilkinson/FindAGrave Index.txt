Spine Injury Serious

Greensburg, Pa.. July 22, 1926 The condition of Ross R. Wilkinson of Greensburg, A pointere, who is suffering of an injury to the spine while picking cherries, was reported as critical at the Westmoreland hospital this morning. It is believed that he has been fatally hurt. 

Ross was on break from painting. His buddy held the ladder. Ross reached to pick some great cherries. Lost his balance and fell on his head. Ross died a few days later.

New Castle News Thursday July 22, 1926
