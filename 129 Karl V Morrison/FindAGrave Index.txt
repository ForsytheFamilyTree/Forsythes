Carl Morrison Obituary

Services for Carl Morrison, 65, of 1516 W. 3rd St., Davenport, will be 11 a.m. Tuesday
at Weerts/Hill & Fredericks Funeral Home, Davenport. Burial will be in Davenport Memorial Park.

Visitation is 2 to 5 and 7 to 9 p.m. today.

Memorials may be made to Cedar Memorial Christian Church, Davenport, or the Lung Association.

Mr. Morrison died Saturday at Mercy Hospital.

He was employed at John Deere Plow & Planter Works, Moline, for 29 1/2 years, retiring in 1972.

He married Thelma Morgan in 1954 in Davenport.

Survivors include his wife; stepdaughters, June Anderson, Sierra Vista, Ariz., and Mrs. Laverne
(Doris) Paulson, Madison, Wis.; stepsons, Harold Morgan, Duane Morgan and Floyd Morgan, all of
Madison, and Louis Morgan, Boise, Idaho; 21 grandchildren; 15 great grandchildren; a sister,
Lorene Showman, Oskaloosa, Iowa; brothers, Floyd, Oskaloosa, and Fallace, Phoenix, Ariz.
