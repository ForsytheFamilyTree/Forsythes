Guy Alvia Morrison is the ninth-born child of John Thomas and Nancy Rebeca. 
This is the wedding picture for Guy and first wife Lovina "Louia" Ellen Jones.  They had two
sons, Raymond Earl and Emery Leroy, before divorcing circa 1925.
