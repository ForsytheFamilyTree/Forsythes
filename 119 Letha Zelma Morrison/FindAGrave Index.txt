OBITUARY

Letha Zelma Morrison, daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Tenny Morrison of Bunch, died Feb. 29, 1912,
age 4 years, 6 months. Funeral services were held at the Pleasant Hill Christian church March
2nd, 1912, conducted by E. A. Hastings of Floris. Zelma was a beautiful and lovable child and
will be sadly missed by her parents and relatives. Their sorrow is not without hope. Jesus said
"of such is the Kingdom of Heaven."
