FLOYD W. MORRISON

Floyd W. Morrison, 82, of 401 North 2nd Street, Oskaloosa, died Tuesday, Sept. 20, 1988 at
Iowa Methodist Medical Center in Des Moines. He was born Nov. 20, 1905 in Davis County, the
son of John and Florence Elder Morrison.

When he was a small boy, his parents moved to a farm near Searsboro where he attended school
and graduated with the class of 1924. On Aug. 20, 1927 he married Glenna Robertson at Ottumwa.
They lived most of the 61 years of their married life in Oskaloosa.

He was a retired machinist after working with the Iowa Valve Company for many years. Later, he
became a realtor and an insurance agent for American Family Insurance. He was a member of the
First Presbyterian Church in Oskaloosa. He is survived by his wife, Glenna of Oskaloosa, two
sons, and four grandchildren.

He was preceded in death by his parents, an infant son, two brothers, and three sisters.

Funeral services will be held Friday at 11 a.m. at the Newman Funeral Chapel with Rev. Peter
Fribley officiating. Burial will be in the Forest Cemetery in Oskaloosa.

Memorials may be made to the American Heart Association.

The family will be present at the funeral home from 7 to 8 p.m. Thursday to greet friends and
relatives.

The Newman Funeral Chapel is in charge of the arrangements.

From the Oskaloosa Daily Herald September 21, 1988 issue
